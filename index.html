Burglar Time

Congratulations on sneaking your way into the vault! There's plenty of $$$ to collect, but you have to get it all and get back out without getting caught by the guards patrolling the maze.

Oh, yeah - the vault door locked behind you, and it won't open up until you've collected all the coins and bills. Once you've collected everything, the vault door will open, and you can escape. Isn't technology strange?

One more thing - if you don't do it in 90 seconds, you'll be trapped and the guards will get you. I'm sure you'll be fine!

Here's a helpful tip: when you collect the bill stacks, you'll set off an alarm that'll make the guards stop for a few seconds and try to track on their GPS devices. So use that to your advantage!

Anyway...be quick, but be careful! There's no "extra lives" when you're on the run
